who are we?

We are students at the University of Bahrain majoring in Arts and Design.

What is our idea?

At Seffa Company, we create innovative furniture pieces, inspired by palm trees, as the company intends to revive Bahrain’s heritage by using palm fronds and inserting them into furniture pieces, where a unique and real aesthetic touch is added, making it sustainable furniture with cultural value.

Our product

 

Seffa table & chair

The simple design of a table with a chair creates an aesthetic image taken from Bahraini heritage. The durable and solid materials make it easy to handle and have a strong, solid structure. It has removable glass and can be switched between the chair and the table in moments - and you will always have extra spacious storage space in the table's cavity in which you can put some of the things you want.

 

Made of multiple materials, including iron, wood, glass, and some parts of palm trees, it gives an aesthetic and innovative touch to the world of furniture.
